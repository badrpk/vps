### **Primary Segments**

#### **Segment 1: Mid-Market Enterprises**
- **Size**: 100-1000 employees
- **Revenue**: $10M-$100M
- **Pain Points**: Vendor lock-in, complex pricing, limited flexibility
- **Acquisition Channels**: Direct sales, partner channel, events
- **CAC Target**: $3,000
- **CLV Target**: $25,000

#### **Segment 2: Startups & Scale-ups**
- **Size**: 10-100 employees
- **Revenue**: $1M-$10M
- **Pain Points**: High costs, complex setup, limited resources
- **Acquisition Channels**: Digital marketing, self-service, content
- **CAC Target**: $1,000
- **CLV Target**: $15,000

- **Size**: 20-200 employees
- **Revenue**: $5M-$50M
- **Pain Points**: Infrastructure complexity, vendor dependency

- **CAC Target**: $2,000
